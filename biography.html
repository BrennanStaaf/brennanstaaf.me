Brennan was born and raised in Erie, Pennsylvania, where he graduated from Merychurst Preparatory School in May 2015. He got his start performing at the Erie Playhouse. He is a rising junior at the University of Miami and in May 2019 he will graduate with a B.F.A. in Musical Theatre. When not performing, he likes to bake French macarons, tap it back at SoulCycle, and live life to the fullest!
